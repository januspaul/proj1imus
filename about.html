Author

This website was created due to my hobbies, a bout of boredom, and the need to pump out a project for my web development class. I wanted this website to look clean, and as much as possible look like a book. So I decided to use the color white as a focus, and also because I can't design a beautiful website to save myself.


I quite like how it turned out, if I do say so myself. Very utilitarian with little to no flair at all. It just gets in your face, shows you what it does, then leaves you to it. I mean, I don't need to re-invent the wheel right? I made the website like this and it works. The content presents itself clearly, without and fanfare and leaves itself to be seen, not hiding anything.


This website tries to cater to people who likes to watch movies, which is everyone. Be it fantasy, reality, fact or fiction we've got you covered.
